Through this quantitative portfolio analysis, I developed strong skills in financial data analysis, including calculating and interpreting returns, variance, and standard deviation. I learned to assess risk using the Sharpe ratio and evaluate relationships between assets through covariance and correlation analysis. I gained experience in hypothesis testing with chi-square and F-tests to compare return variability, and I applied regression modeling to examine how individual stock performance relates to overall market movements. This project strengthened my ability to interpret statistical results, make data-driven investment insights, and understand portfolio diversification, risk management, and the dynamics of financial markets.
